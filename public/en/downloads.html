About the CSV format

If a member has more than one phone number or email, they're separated by a pipe character (|) in the phones and emails columns, e.g. 9851012345|9801234567. In Excel or Google Sheets this shows up as a single cell; use "Text to Columns" (delimiter: |) to split multiple values. For structured use, members.json is recommended, where these fields are available as real arrays.

Terms of use

This data was compiled from the Federal Parliament Secretariat's official Telephone Directory (2083) and hr.parliament.gov.np and na.parliament.gov.np. It's available free and open-source. Please read about the data's reliability before formal use.
