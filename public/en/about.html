About this directory

Transparency is our priority. Here are full details on how the data was collected and processed.

What this is

The "Federal Parliament Contact Directory" is a civic-tech project that brings together contact details for all 332 honorable members of Nepal's House of Representatives (275 members) and National Assembly (57 members) in one place. It also includes contacts for leadership (the Speaker, Chairperson, Prime Minister) and 16 subject-area committees.

How the data was collected

The original source was the Federal Parliament Secretariat's "Telephone Directory 2083" (a 106-page PDF). A font-encoding error in this PDF meant direct text extraction confused certain Devanagari characters (such as "म" and "ह"). To fix this:

  • Numeric/Latin data such as phone numbers and emails were taken directly from the PDF's text layer (these are reliable).
  • Member names and districts were cross-checked and corrected against the official English/Devanagari rosters at hr.parliament.gov.np and na.parliament.gov.np.
  • Profile photos were sourced from those same official websites.
  • Party names were standardized and classified into short codes (RSP, NC, UML, etc.).

Known limitations

  • 8 of the 332 members' phone numbers were not available in the source directory, so they were left blank rather than guessed.
  • National Assembly serial number 14 is absent from the original PDF itself (not an error on our part; that's how the source document is).
  • Some committee secretary names are still pending full verification (flagged with a ⚠ on the Leadership page).
  • This is an independent civic-tech project. It is not the official website of the Federal Parliament Secretariat.

Open-source data

All data can be downloaded for free in CSV and JSON formats.
